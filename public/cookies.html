Welcome to Golden Sport Live. This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website goldensport.vercel.app.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help improve your browsing experience. They allow our website to remember your preferences and provide faster and personalized content.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Save your browsing preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Display relevant sports content

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Needed for website performance and security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site.
  • Preference Cookies: Save your settings like theme or favorites.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics or AdSense to collect anonymous data for improving performance.

5. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, some features on our site may not work properly without them.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page.
